Accessibility

At Café Roya we aim to cater to our customers every need.

We have a ramp for wheelchair access as well as a disabled bathroom located on the ground floor.

If you have any special needs during your visit, please don’t hesitate to give us a call on 0115 922 1902 and we will do our best to cater for you.
