Welcome!

Café Roya is a cosy plant-based eatery located in the heart of Beeston. We specialise in vegetarian and vegan cuisine, serving to every palette and dietary need.

From hearty mains to delicate desserts, all of our dishes are stacked with mouth-watering flavours and are suitable for vegetarians and non-vegetarians alike.

Sustainability is our ethos, for both environment and community. We responsibly source everything that we use—from our fresh fruit and vegetables courtesy of local greengrocer Fred Hallam, to our environmentally-friendly cleaning products from Lembas.

Contact

Accessibility

At Café Roya we aim to cater to our customers every need.

We have a ramp for wheelchair access as well as a disabled bathroom located on the ground floor.

If you have any special needs during your visit, please don’t hesitate to give us a call on 0115 922 1902 and we will do our best to cater for you.
